## Dragon Blue Wyrmling

*Medium dragon, lawful evil*

**Hit Dice** 8 (100 8d8+8)

**Armor Class** 17 (natural armor)

| STR     | DEX     | CON     | INT     | WIS     | CHA     |
|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|---------|
| 17 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 15 (+1) | 12 (+0) | 11 (+0) | 15 (+1) |

**Saves** Physical 14, Evasion 15, Mental 14, Luck 15

**Skills** Stab 2, Notice 2, Sneak 1

**Move** 30 ft., burrow 15 ft., fly 60 ft.

**Morale** 9

## Attacks

***Bite.*** +3 (Stab 2 STR +1), reach 5 ft., one target. Hit: 1d10+1 piercing damage plus 3 (1d6) lightning damage.

***Lightning Breath (Recharge 5-6).*** The dragon exhales lightning in a 30-foot line that is 5 feet wide. Each creature in that line must make a DC 12 Dexterity saving throw, taking 22 (4d10) lightning damage on a failed save, or half as much damage on a successful one.
